About Me

Keep moving forward. A simple phrase, yet it provides a strong message towards the future, perseverance, and growth. If this were not ingrained in my head, then I wouldn't be where I am today - on the path to becoming the best full-stack developer I can be.

I initially pursued a bachelor's degree in business with a double concentration in management and supply chain operations at Californiate State University, Long Beach with the aim to assist professionals in finding effective and efficient solutions. Soon after graduation, I was able to find my first job as a client service administrator at a bank. As with all my other jobs, I was on the search for what more I can do in the position I'm in. On top of my primary responsibilities, I was asked to help in the technology side to automate bank transfers. During this, I was exposed to coding through Microsoft Excel VBA and ended up creating macros to automate my own tasks, as well as my coworkers which helped reduce workloads by about 10%.

I found that I wanted to delve deeper into coding and started learning on my own time through different tools such as uDemy, and freecodecamp. Although I enjoyed this self learning experience, I felt I was able to make more progress through a dedicated learning environment which is how I stumbled upon the coding bootcamp, LearningFuze. I recently gradauted and found it to be what I desired and more. Not only was I exposed to more technologies, such as Node, React, and PostgreSQL, but I was constantly challenged to find the solution myself with just the right amount of assistance from the instructors. We had daily exercises that helped build our foundation of programming and multiple projects to foster our knowledge of real world application. Another part of the curriculum that I throughly enjoyed was that it was simulated similiar to a work environment through GitHub where we had to add, commit, and push to GitHub and get our issues approved before merging and moving on. I learned that part of being a developer also meant that you'll need to find the solutions with the resources you have whether it be through documentation, online forums, or asking someone with the knowledge you're looking for.

With my combined experience from LearningFuze and bachelor's degree in business, I am confident that I can provide value to your team. As a team player with a passion and appreciation for coding/technology, I would love to show you these first hand at your company.
